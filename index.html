Welcome Adventurer

Before you begin your journey, pick your preferred race. Each race has 3 stats: strength, intelligence, and luck. The stats will be randomly generated from a range depending on the class.

This "Choose Your Own Adventure" has RNG aspects. Some choices throughout the game will have a chance of success. Some choice will scale off strength, intelligence, or luck. The probability and the stat is scales off with will be given. Most of the time, it will scale off of strength. (Having higher strength will increase the chances of success.) Higher intelligence will increase the amount of information that you will get throughout your journey. Luck increases the chance of random events
