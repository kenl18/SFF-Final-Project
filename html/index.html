Welcome Adventurer!

Before you start your journey, pick your preferred class. Each class has 3 stats: strength, intelligence, and luck.

  • Strength: Increases the chance of success in battles.
  • Intelligence: Increases perception and information
  • Luck: Increases the chance of encounters

Each stat will be randomly generated depending on the class chosen. Minimum stat is 0, while the maximum is 20.
