You just noticed that you had been working for the whole day without rest, water, or food. Unfortunately, you were unable to stop yourself from falling in the ocean and knocking yourself out.

While you were knocked out, a swarm of fish led by the fish you tried to catch earlier in the day came to you. They began to bite all over your body. The ocean began to be tainted with red liquid as your blood flowed out of you in buckets. You didn't wake up the next day nor as your body able to be found floating in the sea.
