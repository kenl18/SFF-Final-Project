You decide to take the risk and open the door. You open the door slowly in hopes of catching how the stranger looked. You couldn't make out the shape of the traveler, so you decided to open the door fully. Tears of cold rain slowly begin to enter and the hinge squeaked with each angle increase. When the door was open to its full glory, lightning flashed illuminating the silhouette of the dripping visitor.

He was fully covered in a cloak that covered his face all the way down to his knees. The cloak was ragged as if it has stood the testament of time and precipitation. The traveler stood at the same height as you. He gave off a gentle aura: one that was seemed to be truthful in nature.

You welcome this person inside, sat him down, and prepared a nice hot cup of tea to warm him up. When the stranger took off his cloak to drink, you were shocked by how young he looked. Perhaps, young and old can't seem to describe the stature of his face. His hair and beard, both white in color, were overgrown all over the place. Despite all this, his face was rather free of wrinkles, and looked regal indeed.

"What brought you to be my place and why are you traveling in this kind of weather?" you asked.

There was a moment of awkward silence as the mysterious person finished drinking the tea. He let out a sigh and began to talk. "Thank you. My name is FriedSieg and I'm on a quest. It's an important quest after all and I can't let a storm slow me down. Now that this tempest has escalated rather quickly, I politely ask for your hospitality."

"What an interesting request that is. Should I allow it?"
