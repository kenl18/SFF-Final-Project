The two of you decide to go the tavern, because there had been rumors that the bartender is able to serve up drinks that has random effects. The bartender doesn't seem to control what effects the drink gives. It can either give you a stat increase or a decrease.

You walk inside of the bar and the bartender promptly delivers you a drink. Now, you can decide whether or not you are going to drink it. If you don't choose to drink it, we are move on to the dragon fight. Otherwise, good luck.
